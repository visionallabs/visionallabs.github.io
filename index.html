Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What's with all the dragons?

The founders are Welsh. If you've seen their flag, then you know. The Ddraig Goch is prominent. Cymru Am Byth!

Q. Is it true that your CEO is secretly a robot programmed to write bug-free code?

We don't have a CEO, but if we did, they would promptly be replaced with a Bash script.

Q. Is it true your tech support team consists entirely of highly trained cats?

Look up. Look down. You look like you may be tasty with ketchup. Whataburger ketchup. The spicy kind.

Q. Will using your software make me a coding ninja or just really good at procrastinating?

We'll get back to you on that. One day.

Q. Is your open-source code powered by coffee, tea, or pure programmer adrenaline?

None of the above. It is fueled by the chilled tears of Emacs users.

Q. What's the official company stance on whether a hot dog is a sandwich?

No. We've been to New York City. We've been to Chicago. We've even been to Pink's in LA. They are works of art. Stop thy devilry.
